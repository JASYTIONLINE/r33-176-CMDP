SUBJECT: Battery Maintenance Operator Appointment

1. In accordance with TB 43-0134 and DA Pam 750-1, the following individuals are appointed as Battery Maintenance Operators for [UNIT NAME].

   a. **Primary:** [RANK, NAME]  
   b. **Alternate:** [RANK, NAME]

2. **Authority.** TB 43-0134, Battery Disposition and Disposal; DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.

3. **Effective Date.** [DD MONTH YYYY].

4. **Period.** Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.

5. **Duties and Responsibilities.** Battery Maintenance Operators will:
   - Test batteries IAW TB 43-0134 using approved testing equipment
   - Maintain battery maintenance records and documentation
   - Ensure proper storage of serviceable and unserviceable batteries
   - Conduct battery charging operations in designated areas
   - Use required PPE during all battery operations
   - Maintain battery maintenance area cleanliness and safety
   - Coordinate turn-in of unserviceable batteries
   - Maintain spill response equipment and procedures
   - Train operators on basic battery care and maintenance

6. **Qualifications.** Must have completed battery maintenance training and be familiar with TB 43-0134 requirements.

7. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.

8. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].
