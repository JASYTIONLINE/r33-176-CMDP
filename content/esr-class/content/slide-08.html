Case Study: Manpower or Supply?

This slide asks the class to describe the equipment, the fault, and the problem before deciding what is really holding up progress. The case study format encourages learners to slow down and read the ESR entry instead of jumping straight to an assumption.

The central question is whether the delay is caused by manpower, supply, or another execution issue. Leaders should use the ESR to separate symptoms from root causes, then communicate the correct priority and follow-up action to the people who can move the repair forward.
