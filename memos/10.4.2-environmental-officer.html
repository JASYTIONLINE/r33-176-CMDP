MEMORANDUM FOR SEE DISTRIBUTION

SUBJECT: Environmental Officer Appointment

  1. In accordance with AR 200-1 and DA Pam 750-1, the following individuals are appointed as the Environmental Officer for [UNIT NAME].

a. Primary: [RANK, NAME]

b. Alternate: [RANK, NAME]

  1. Authority. AR 200-1, Environmental Protection and Enhancement; DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.
  1. Effective Date. [DD MONTH YYYY].
  1. Period. Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.
  1. Duties and Responsibilities. The Environmental Officer will:
  • Serve as the commander's principal advisor on environmental matters
  • Ensure compliance with federal, state, and Army environmental regulations
  • Manage hazardous material (HAZMAT) and hazardous waste programs
  • Conduct environmental inspections of unit facilities
  • Maintain environmental records and documentation
  • Coordinate spill prevention and response procedures
  • Ensure proper storage and disposal of hazardous materials
  • Conduct environmental awareness training
  • Coordinate with installation environmental office
  1. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.
  1. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].

Respectfully,
