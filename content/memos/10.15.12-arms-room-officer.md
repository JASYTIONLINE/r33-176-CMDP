SUBJECT: Arms Room Officer Appointment

1. In accordance with AR 190-11, AR 710-2, and DA Pam 750-1, the following individual is appointed as the Arms Room Officer for [UNIT NAME].

   **Arms Room Officer:** [RANK, NAME]

2. **Authority.** AR 190-11, Physical Security of Arms, Ammunition, and Explosives; AR 710-2, Supply Policy Below the National Level; DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.

3. **Effective Date.** [DD MONTH YYYY].

4. **Period.** Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.

5. **Duties and Responsibilities.** The Arms Room Officer will:
   - Provide officer oversight of the arms room program
   - Conduct monthly inspections of the arms room
   - Review and validate weapons inventory documentation
   - Ensure arms room security standards are maintained
   - Review arms room SOP for compliance with regulations
   - Brief commander on arms room status and issues
   - Coordinate weapons maintenance requirements
   - Investigate and report weapons discrepancies
   - Ensure armorer training and certification is current
   - Supervise arms room operations

6. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.

7. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].
