SUBJECT: CBRN Officer Appointment

1. In accordance with AR 350-1, FM 3-11, and DA Pam 750-1, the following individual is appointed as the CBRN Officer for [UNIT NAME].

   **CBRN Officer:** [RANK, NAME]

2. **Authority.** AR 350-1, Army Training and Leader Development; FM 3-11, CBRN Operations; DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.

3. **Effective Date.** [DD MONTH YYYY].

4. **Period.** Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.

5. **Duties and Responsibilities.** The CBRN Officer will:
   - Serve as the commander's principal officer advisor on CBRN matters
   - Provide officer oversight of the unit CBRN program
   - Review and validate CBRN training plans and schedules
   - Ensure CBRN equipment readiness and accountability
   - Brief commander on CBRN status and issues
   - Coordinate CBRN requirements with higher headquarters
   - Review CBRN SOP for compliance with regulations
   - Supervise CBRN Primary and CBRN Assistants
   - Ensure integration of CBRN into unit training plans
   - Represent the unit on CBRN matters at staff meetings

6. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.

7. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].
