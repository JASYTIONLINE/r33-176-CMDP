MEMORANDUM FOR RECORD

SUBJECT: Unit Battery Maintenance Program Establishment

1. **Purpose.** This memorandum establishes the Battery Maintenance Program for [UNIT NAME] in accordance with TB 43-0134 and DA Pam 750-1.

2. **Authority.** TB 43-0134, Battery Disposition and Disposal; DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.

3. **Effective Date.** [DD MONTH YYYY].

4. **Program Requirements.** The unit will maintain a battery maintenance program that includes:

   a. **Designated Battery Maintenance Area.** Location: [BUILDING/AREA]
   
   b. **Battery Testing.** All batteries will be tested IAW TB 43-0134 using approved battery testing equipment.
   
   c. **Battery Storage.** Batteries will be stored in approved battery storage containers/racks with proper ventilation.
   
   d. **Personal Protective Equipment (PPE).** The following PPE will be available at all battery maintenance areas:
      - Acid-resistant apron
      - Face shield or safety goggles
      - Acid-resistant gloves
      - Emergency eyewash station within 25 feet
   
   e. **Spill Response.** Battery acid spill kits will be maintained at all battery work areas.
   
   f. **Charging Operations.** Battery charging will only be conducted in designated, ventilated areas.
   
   g. **Turn-in Procedures.** Unserviceable batteries will be turned in IAW local installation procedures.

5. **Documentation.** Battery test records will be maintained IAW AR 25-400-2.

6. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].
