What Does the ESR Do?

The Equipment Status Report provides a snapshot of equipment readiness in the unit by consolidating faults, work order status, and operational condition into one report. Each line represents a piece of equipment and gives leaders a way to understand what is down, why it is down, and what action is underway.

The ESR is not just an administrative report; it is a decision-making tool. When leaders and maintenance personnel pull and interpret their own data, they gain real-time awareness, set priorities, follow up on repairs, and drive readiness instead of waiting for someone else to explain the maintenance picture.
