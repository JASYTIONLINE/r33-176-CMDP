SUBJECT: Night Vision Device (NVD) Instructor Appointment

  1. In accordance with AR 600-55, TC 21-305-20, and DA Pam 750-1, the following individuals are appointed as Night Vision Device (NVD) Instructors for [UNIT NAME].

a. [RANK, NAME]

b. [RANK, NAME]

c. [RANK, NAME]

  1. Authority. AR 600-55, The Army Driver and Operator Standardization Program; TC 21-305-20, Night Vision Device Driving Operations; DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.
  1. Effective Date. [DD MONTH YYYY].
  1. Period. Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.
  1. Duties and Responsibilities. NVD Instructors will:
  • Conduct NVD operator training IAW TC 21-305-20
  • Train operators on proper NVD use, care, and maintenance
  • Instruct operators on NVD driving techniques and safety
  • Conduct classroom and practical NVD training
  • Prepare operators for NVD licensing examinations
  • Document NVD training in operator training records
  • Ensure training meets all regulatory requirements
  • Coordinate NVD training schedules with Master Driver
  1. Qualifications. Must possess a valid NVD operator license and have completed NVD Instructor certification training.
  1. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.
  1. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].

Respectfully,

[LAST NAME, FIRST NAME MI.]

[RANK, BRANCH, COMPONENT]
