SUBJECT: License Examiner Appointment

1. In accordance with AR 600-55 and DA Pam 750-1, the following individuals are appointed as License Examiners for [UNIT NAME].

   a. [RANK, NAME] — Authorized to examine on: [EQUIPMENT TYPES]  
   b. [RANK, NAME] — Authorized to examine on: [EQUIPMENT TYPES]  
   c. [RANK, NAME] — Authorized to examine on: [EQUIPMENT TYPES]

2. **Authority.** AR 600-55, The Army Driver and Operator Standardization Program (Selection, Training, Testing, and Licensing); DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.

3. **Effective Date.** [DD MONTH YYYY].

4. **Period.** Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.

5. **Duties and Responsibilities.** License Examiners will:
   - Administer operator licensing examinations IAW AR 600-55
   - Conduct written and practical tests for licensing
   - Evaluate operator proficiency on assigned equipment
   - Complete and sign DA Form 348 (Equipment Operator's Qualification Record)
   - Ensure examination standards meet regulatory requirements
   - Document examination results and failures
   - Coordinate examination schedules with Master Driver
   - Maintain objectivity and integrity in all examinations

6. **Qualifications.** Must be a licensed operator on all equipment types they examine and have completed License Examiner training. License Examiners will not examine operators they have trained.

7. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.

8. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].
