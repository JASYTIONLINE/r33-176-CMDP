## 1. PURPOSE

This SOP establishes procedures for the management, control, and maintenance of technical publications within [UNIT NAME] in accordance with AR 25-30 and DA Pam 750-1.

---

## 2. APPLICABILITY

This SOP applies to all personnel assigned or attached to [UNIT NAME] who use, maintain, or manage technical publications.

---

## 3. REFERENCES

- AR 25-30, The Army Publishing Program
- DA Pam 25-30, Consolidated Index of Army Publications and Blank Forms
- DA Pam 750-1, Commanders' Maintenance Handbook
- DA Pam 25-33, User's Guide for Army Publications and Forms

---

## 4. RESPONSIBILITIES

### 4.1 Commander
- Ensures publications program compliance
- Appoints Publications Coordinator if required
- Provides resources for publications management

### 4.2 Publications Coordinator / Maintenance Officer
- Manages unit publications program
- Ensures publications are current
- Conducts publications inventories

### 4.3 All Personnel
- Use current publications only
- Report missing or damaged publications
- Return publications when no longer needed

---

## 5. PROCEDURES

### 5.1 Publications Requirements
a. Identify required publications based on assigned equipment  
b. Reference DA Pam 25-30 for current publications  
c. Determine format requirements (electronic or paper)  
d. Request publications through proper channels

### 5.2 Publications Sources
a. **Electronic Publications:**
   - Army Publishing Directorate (APD) website
   - Logistics Information Warehouse (LIW)
   - GCSS-Army technical data

b. **Paper Publications:**
   - Order through publications account
   - Request from supporting publications office

### 5.3 Publications Currency
a. **Verification:**
   - Check APD website monthly for updates
   - Review DA Pam 25-30 for current editions
   - Verify Modification Work Orders (MWOs) are current

b. **Updates:**
   - Apply changes and revisions promptly
   - Remove superseded pages/publications
   - Document updates in publications record

c. **MWO Compliance:**
   - Review MWOs for assigned equipment
   - Document MWO application status
   - Coordinate with maintenance for MWO application

### 5.4 Publications Storage and Control
a. Store publications in accessible location  
b. Protect from damage and environmental factors  
c. Maintain publications library inventory  
d. Control access to classified publications IAW security requirements

### 5.5 Publications Inventories
a. Conduct quarterly inventories  
b. Verify publications are current editions  
c. Identify missing or damaged publications  
d. Document inventory results

### 5.6 Lubrication Orders (LOs)
a. Maintain current LO for each equipment type  
b. Post LO at operator/maintenance level  
c. Verify LO compliance during PMCS  
d. Update LOs when revised

---

## 6. REQUIRED PUBLICATIONS

For each assigned equipment type, the unit will maintain:
- Operator's Manual (TM -10)
- Unit Maintenance Manual (TM -23&P)
- Lubrication Order (LO)
- Applicable TBs and MWOs

---

## 7. RECORDS AND REPORTS

- Publications inventory list
- Publications request documentation
- MWO status records
- Change/revision logs

---

## 8. ELECTRONIC PUBLICATIONS ACCESS

- Ensure computer access to APD website
- Bookmark frequently used publications
- Download publications for offline use as needed
- Verify electronic access during field operations
