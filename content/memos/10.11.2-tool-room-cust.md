SUBJECT: Tool Room Custodian Appointment

1. In accordance with AR 710-2, DA Pam 750-1, and CTA 50-970, the following individuals are appointed as Tool Room Custodian for [UNIT NAME].

   a. **Primary:** [RANK, NAME]  
   b. **Alternate:** [RANK, NAME]

2. **Authority.** AR 710-2, Supply Policy Below the National Level; DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10; CTA 50-970, Expendable/Durable Items.

3. **Effective Date.** [DD MONTH YYYY].

4. **Period.** Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.

5. **Duties and Responsibilities.** The Tool Room Custodian will:
   - Maintain accountability of all tools and tool sets
   - Conduct monthly inventories of tool room contents
   - Ensure tools are properly stored, maintained, and secured
   - Manage tool sign-out/sign-in procedures
   - Maintain tool room hand receipts and documentation
   - Report missing, damaged, or unserviceable tools
   - Coordinate tool replacement and procurement
   - Ensure proper tool room security (locks, access control)
   - Conduct joint inventories during custodian changes

6. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.

7. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].
