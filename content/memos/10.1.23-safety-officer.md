SUBJECT: Safety Officer Appointment

1. In accordance with AR 385-10 and DA Pam 750-1, the following individuals are appointed as the Safety Officer/NCO for [UNIT NAME].

   a. **Primary:** [RANK, NAME]  
   b. **Alternate:** [RANK, NAME]

2. **Authority.** AR 385-10, The Army Safety Program; DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.

3. **Effective Date.** [DD MONTH YYYY].

4. **Period.** Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.

5. **Duties and Responsibilities.** The Safety Officer/NCO will:
   - Serve as the commander's principal advisor on safety matters
   - Conduct safety inspections of maintenance facilities and operations
   - Ensure compliance with OSHA and Army safety regulations
   - Investigate accidents and near-misses
   - Maintain safety records and statistics
   - Conduct safety briefings and training
   - Ensure proper PPE availability and usage
   - Review SOPs for safety compliance
   - Coordinate with higher headquarters safety personnel

6. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.

7. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].
