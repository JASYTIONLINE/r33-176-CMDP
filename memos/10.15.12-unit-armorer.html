MEMORANDUM FOR SEE DISTRIBUTION

SUBJECT: Unit Armorer Appointment

  1. In accordance with AR 190-11, AR 710-2, and DA Pam 750-1, the following individual is appointed as the Unit Armorer for [UNIT NAME].

Primary Armorer: [RANK, NAME]

  1. Authority. AR 190-11, Physical Security of Arms, Ammunition, and Explosives; AR 710-2, Supply Policy Below the National Level; DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.
  1. Effective Date. [DD MONTH YYYY].
  1. Period. Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.
  1. Duties and Responsibilities. The Unit Armorer will:
  • Maintain accountability of all weapons assigned to the unit
  • Conduct weapons inventories IAW AR 190-11
  • Perform weapons maintenance and repairs within unit capability
  • Maintain weapons maintenance records and documentation
  • Ensure arms room security standards are met
  • Conduct weapons inspections and serviceability checks
  • Coordinate with Direct Support for weapons repairs beyond unit capability
  • Issue and receive weapons IAW established procedures
  • Maintain arms room logs and access rosters
  • Ensure proper storage of cleaning materials and supplies
  1. Qualifications. Must have completed Unit Armorer Course or equivalent training.
  1. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.
  1. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].

Respectfully,

[LAST NAME, FIRST NAME MI.]

[RANK, BRANCH, COMPONENT]

Commanding
