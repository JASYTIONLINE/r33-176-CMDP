Questions

This section concludes the training on reading and understanding the ESR. Leaders are expected to apply the concepts from the presentation to take ownership of their maintenance programs and drive readiness within their units.

Questions are encouraged so participants can clarify their understanding before they put these practices into action. The goal is for every learner to leave prepared to read the ESR, communicate priorities, follow up on problems, and use the report as a leadership tool.
