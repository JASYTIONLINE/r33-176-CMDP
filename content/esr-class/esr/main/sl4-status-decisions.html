ESR Turns Status Into Decisions

The ESR is a powerful tool for understanding the current readiness picture. It shows leaders which assets are down, what faults are driving the status, and how each issue affects the unit's ability to support the mission.

It also shows whether the problem is tied to repair parts, manpower, or lack of follow-through. This distinction matters, because each problem requires a different action, or a different conversation with different members of the maintenance team.

Applying pressure to a situation only works when it is applied to the right place. Pushing a support agency to fix equipment without available parts wastes effort, but taking action to independently source parts, reallocate manpower assets, or readjust priorities can move the repair process forward.
