MEMORANDUM FOR SEE DISTRIBUTION

SUBJECT: Maintenance Officer Appointment

  1. In accordance with AR 750-1 and DA Pam 750-1, the following individual is appointed as the Maintenance Officer for [UNIT NAME].

a. Primary: [RANK, NAME]

b. Alternate: [RANK, NAME]

  1. Authority. AR 750-1, The Army Maintenance Management System (TAMMS); DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.
  1. Effective Date. [DD MONTH YYYY].
  1. Period. Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.
  1. Duties and Responsibilities. The Maintenance Officer will:
  • Serve as the commander's principal advisor on all maintenance matters
  • Ensure maintenance operations comply with AR 750-1 and DA Pam 750-1
  • Supervise unit maintenance activities and personnel
  • Monitor equipment readiness and maintenance status
  • Coordinate maintenance support requirements
  • Review and validate maintenance requests
  • Ensure proper use of GCSS-Army for maintenance management
  1. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.
  1. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].
