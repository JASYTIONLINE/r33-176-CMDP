SUBJECT: Key Custodian Appointment

1. In accordance with AR 190-11 and DA Pam 750-1, the following individuals are appointed as Key Custodian for [UNIT NAME].

   a. **Primary:** [RANK, NAME]  
   b. **Alternate:** [RANK, NAME]

2. **Authority.** AR 190-11, Physical Security of Arms, Ammunition, and Explosives; DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.

3. **Effective Date.** [DD MONTH YYYY].

4. **Period.** Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.

5. **Duties and Responsibilities.** The Key Custodian will:
   - Maintain accountability of all keys assigned to the unit
   - Maintain key control register and issue logs
   - Ensure keys are properly secured when not in use
   - Issue and receive keys IAW established procedures
   - Conduct periodic inventories of all keys
   - Report lost or compromised keys immediately
   - Coordinate key replacement and rekeying as needed
   - Ensure key control cabinet/safe is properly secured
   - Maintain key control SOP compliance

6. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.

7. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].
