Required SOPs (FY24–25)

This page lists all mandatory SOPs explicitly required by the CMDP Checklist.

Missing any SOP listed below results in an automatic NO-GO during inspection.

Each SOP will be its own page with a downloadable template.

A. General Operations

  • Maintenance SOP

*Required by checklist lines referencing AR 750-1 and DA PAM 750-3 for unit maintenance operations.*

J-Series (Drivers & Operators Program)

  • Driver Training SOP

*Covers selection, training, testing, licensing, sustainment requirements.*

Environmental & HAZMAT Management

  • Environmental / HAZMAT SOP

*Supports compliance with AR 200-1 and CMDP environmental requirements.*

Battery Maintenance Program

  • Battery Maintenance SOP

*Defines battery storage, testing, turn-in, PPE, and handling operations.*

Arms Room / Weapons Maintenance

  • Arms Room / Weapons Maintenance SOP

*Required for weapon cleaning, PMCS, documentation, safety, and maintenance standards.*

CBRN Operations

  • CBRN Operations SOP

*Defines CBRN equipment maintenance, training, and GCSS-Army integration.*

Publications Control

  • Publications SOP

*Required to ensure all TMs, TBs, LOs, and MWOs are current and controlled.*

Tool Room Operations

  • Tool Room SOP

*Covers accountability, sign-out, inventories, security, and storage for tools.*

Dispatch Operations

  • Dispatch SOP

*Required when checklist mandates established dispatch procedures.*
