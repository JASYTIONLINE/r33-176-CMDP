SUBJECT: Radiation Safety Officer (RSO) Appointment

1. In accordance with AR 385-10, AR 11-9, and DA Pam 750-1, the following individual is appointed as the Radiation Safety Officer (RSO) for [UNIT NAME].

   **Radiation Safety Officer:** [RANK, NAME]

2. **Authority.** AR 385-10, The Army Safety Program; AR 11-9, The Army Radiation Safety Program; DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.

3. **Effective Date.** [DD MONTH YYYY].

4. **Period.** Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.

5. **Duties and Responsibilities.** The Radiation Safety Officer will:
   - Serve as the commander's principal advisor on radiation safety matters
   - Ensure compliance with Army radiation safety regulations
   - Maintain accountability of all radiation-emitting items
   - Conduct radiation safety inspections and surveys
   - Maintain radiation exposure records for personnel
   - Ensure proper storage and handling of radioactive materials
   - Coordinate radiation safety training for personnel
   - Report radiation incidents IAW established procedures
   - Maintain required licenses and permits for radioactive materials
   - Coordinate with installation RSO and higher headquarters

6. **Qualifications.** Must have completed Radiation Safety Officer training and certification.

7. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.

8. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].
