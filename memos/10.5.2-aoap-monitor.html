MEMORANDUM FOR SEE DISTRIBUTION

SUBJECT: Army Oil Analysis Program (AOAP) Monitor Appointment

  1. In accordance with AR 750-1, DA Pam 750-1, and TB 43-0211, the following individuals are appointed as the Army Oil Analysis Program (AOAP) Monitor for [UNIT NAME].

a. Primary: [RANK, NAME]

b. Alternate: [RANK, NAME]

  1. Authority. AR 750-1; DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10; TB 43-0211, Army Oil Analysis Program (AOAP).
  1. Effective Date. [DD MONTH YYYY].
  1. Period. Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.
  1. Duties and Responsibilities. The AOAP Monitor will:
  • Manage unit participation in the Army Oil Analysis Program
  • Ensure enrolled equipment is sampled at proper intervals per TB 43-0211
  • Review AOAP laboratory results and initiate corrective actions
  • Maintain AOAP enrollment rosters and sampling schedules
  • Train operators on proper oil sampling procedures
  • Coordinate with supporting AOAP laboratory
  • Track equipment with abnormal oil analysis results
  • Ensure timely response to AOAP exception codes
  • Maintain AOAP records in accordance with AR 25-400-2
  1. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.
  1. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].

Respectfully,

[LAST NAME, FIRST NAME MI.]

[RANK, BRANCH, COMPONENT]

Commanding
