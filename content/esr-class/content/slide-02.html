Why This Training Matters

This slide explains why ESR training is important for unit leadership. The goal is to empower commanders, executive officers, and maintenance leaders to become self-sustaining by analyzing the GCSS-Army data already available to them instead of depending on delayed reports from higher headquarters or supporting agencies.

The ESR matters because it directly informs readiness reporting and operational capability. When leaders understand how to use it, they can identify problems early, track maintenance progress, and know where the unit stands before readiness issues become mission-impacting problems.
