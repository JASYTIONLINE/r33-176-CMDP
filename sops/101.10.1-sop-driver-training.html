1. PURPOSE

This SOP establishes procedures for the selection, training, testing, and licensing of vehicle and equipment operators within [UNIT NAME] in accordance with AR 600-55.

2. APPLICABILITY

This SOP applies to all personnel assigned or attached to [UNIT NAME] who operate, train, test, or license vehicle and equipment operators.

3. REFERENCES

  • AR 600-55, The Army Driver and Operator Standardization Program
  • DA Pam 750-1, Commanders' Maintenance Handbook
  • TC 21-305 Series, Training Circulars for specific equipment
  • Applicable Technical Manuals (TMs) for assigned equipment

4. RESPONSIBILITIES

4.1 Commander

  • Approves operator licensing program
  • Appoints Master Driver, License Instructors, and License Examiners in writing
  • Reviews licensing program status quarterly

4.2 Master Driver

  • Manages the unit driver training and licensing program
  • Supervises License Instructors and License Examiners
  • Maintains training records and documentation

4.3 License Instructors

  • Conduct operator training IAW AR 600-55
  • Document training in DA Form 348
  • Prepare operators for licensing examinations

4.4 License Examiners

  • Administer licensing examinations
  • Evaluate operator proficiency
  • Complete and sign DA Form 348

5. PROCEDURES

5.1 Operator Selection

a. Supervisor nominates personnel for licensing

b. Verify medical fitness (PULHES requirements)

c. Verify valid civilian driver's license if required

d. Commander approves training enrollment

5.2 Training Requirements

a. Classroom Instruction:

  • Vehicle/equipment familiarization
  • PMCS procedures
  • Safety requirements
  • Regulatory requirements

b. Hands-On Training:

  • Minimum hours per AR 600-55 and TCs
  • PMCS performance
  • Operational procedures
  • Emergency procedures

c. Road/Field Training:

  • Supervised operation in training environment
  • Progressive skill development
  • Various terrain and conditions as applicable

5.3 Testing and Licensing

a. Written Examination:

  • Minimum passing score: 70%
  • Covers safety, PMCS, and operational procedures

b. Performance Examination:

  • PMCS performance
  • Equipment operation
  • Evaluated by License Examiner

c. Licensing:

  • Complete DA Form 348
  • Commander signs license
  • Issue OF 346 (U.S. Government Motor Vehicle Operator's Identification Card) if required

5.4 Sustainment Training

a. Annual sustainment training for all licensed operators

b. Document training in DA Form 348

c. Address skill degradation as identified

d. Include safety and regulatory updates

5.5 Night Vision Device (NVD) Operations

a. Separate NVD licensing required

b. Training IAW TC 21-305-20

c. NVD Instructor conducts training

d. Annual NVD sustainment required

6. RECORDS AND REPORTS

  • DA Form 348 (Equipment Operator's Qualification Record)
  • OF 346 (U.S. Government Motor Vehicle Operator's Identification Card)
  • Training attendance rosters
  • Monthly licensing status reports

7. TRAINING DOCUMENTATION

All training will be documented on:

  • DA Form 348 (master record)
  • Unit training calendars
  • Individual training records

8. SAFETY

  • All training conducted IAW safety requirements
  • Instructor present during all hands-on training
  • Required PPE worn during all operations
  • Training vehicles inspected before each training session
