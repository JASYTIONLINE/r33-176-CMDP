SUBJECT: Assistant Armorer Appointment

  1. In accordance with AR 190-11, AR 710-2, and DA Pam 750-1, the following individuals are appointed as Assistant Armorers for [UNIT NAME].

a. [RANK, NAME]

b. [RANK, NAME]

  1. Authority. AR 190-11, Physical Security of Arms, Ammunition, and Explosives; AR 710-2, Supply Policy Below the National Level; DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.
  1. Effective Date. [DD MONTH YYYY].
  1. Period. Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.
  1. Duties and Responsibilities. Assistant Armorers will:
  • Assist the Unit Armorer in all arms room duties
  • Issue and receive weapons in the absence of the Unit Armorer
  • Conduct weapons cleaning and basic maintenance
  • Assist with weapons inventories
  • Maintain arms room security standards
  • Perform duties as assigned by the Unit Armorer
  • Maintain proficiency in weapons maintenance procedures
  • Assist with weapons inspections and serviceability checks
  1. Qualifications. Must have completed Assistant Armorer training or on-the-job training under the Unit Armorer.
  1. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.
  1. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].
