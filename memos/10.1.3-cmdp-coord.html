SUBJECT: Command Maintenance Discipline Program (CMDP) Coordinator Appointment

  1. In accordance with DA Pam 750-1, the following individuals are appointed as the Command Maintenance Discipline Program (CMDP) Coordinators for [UNIT NAME].

a. Primary: [RANK, NAME]

b. Alternate: [RANK, NAME]

  1. Authority. DA Pam 750-1, Paragraph 10-6 (02 February 2023).
  1. Effective Date. [DD MONTH YYYY].
  1. Period. Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.
  1. Duties and Responsibilities. CMDP Coordinators will:
  • Review DA PAM 750-1, Chapter 10, to ensure full understanding of duties and coordination requirements
  • Schedule formal and informal inspections and staff assistance visits as required
  • Coordinate directly with evaluation teams
  • Maintain CMDP inspection records and corrective action plans
  • Conduct quarterly CMDP self-assessments
  • Brief commander on CMDP status and readiness
  • Ensure subordinate units maintain CMDP compliance
  1. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.
