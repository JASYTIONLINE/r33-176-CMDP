Status Codes

Status codes on the ESR show the operational condition of equipment and help explain why an item is not fully mission capable. These codes distinguish between maintenance issues, supply issues, and equipment that is fully operational.

Understanding status codes helps leaders determine whether a problem can be fixed internally, requires parts, or needs external support. Correct interpretation ensures that each piece of equipment receives the right action and that leaders focus attention where it is needed most.
