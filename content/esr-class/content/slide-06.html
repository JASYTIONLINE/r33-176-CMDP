Basic ESR Layout

Interactive: Click any highlighted box on the ESR image to learn what that zone means.

The ESR is structured to present key information in a consistent format, including equipment identifiers, fault descriptions, work order status, and time in status. Understanding where these elements appear on the report is the foundation for reading it correctly.

Leaders should be able to quickly identify what equipment is down, why it is down, and how long it has been in that condition. Once they can read the layout with confidence, they can prioritize work, ask better questions, and make faster maintenance decisions.
