Equipment Status Report Class

This CMDP training introduces leaders and maintenance personnel to the Equipment Status Report, or ESR, as a practical tool for reading, understanding, and acting on maintenance information. The purpose is not only to become familiar with the report, but to build confidence in using it to make informed readiness decisions.

By the end of the class, leaders should be able to independently assess their unit readiness, identify problems early, and take action to improve maintenance outcomes. The opening slide sets the expectation that ESR ownership belongs at the unit level and that leaders should not have to wait on outside reports before acting.
