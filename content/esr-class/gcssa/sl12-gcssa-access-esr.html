How to access the ESR in GCSS-AEK Z-EQUST - Equipment Status Report

This is the GCSS-Army command used to open the Equipment Status Report. Begin here to filter the report to display all Non-Mission Capable Prime Movers in your UIC.

Step 1: Select [Force Element] and enter your unit UIC.

Step 2: In the [Equipment Category] field, enter "C" Fleet-self Prop-RPT(Rolling Stock)

3: Verify the [Equipment Operational Status] field contains "NMC*". * is a wildcard.

Step 4: Verify the [Technical Status] field contains "X" (NMC)and "E" (Administrative Deadline) as shown.

Step 5: Verify Include Suborders is checked to ensure maintenance actions performed by supporting maintenance activities and external shops are included in the report.

Step 6: Execute the report.

The resulting Deadline Report will display all Fleet-self Prop currently in a Non-Mission Capable (NMC) status.
