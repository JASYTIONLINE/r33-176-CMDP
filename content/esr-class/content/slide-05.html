Your Maintenance Fight

This slide frames the ESR as the unit maintenance fight in its entirety. Every fault listed is a problem that must be addressed, and every work order shows either progress or lack of progress toward returning equipment to service.

If equipment appears on the ESR, the unit has a responsibility to act on it. Leaders cannot expect support assets to understand unit priorities unless those priorities are communicated through clear two-way dialogue, regular follow-up, and a shared focus on closing the cycle back to fully mission capable status.
