SUBJECT: CBRN Primary Appointment

1. In accordance with AR 350-1, FM 3-11, and DA Pam 750-1, the following individual is appointed as the CBRN Primary for [UNIT NAME].

   **CBRN Primary:** [RANK, NAME]

2. **Authority.** AR 350-1, Army Training and Leader Development; FM 3-11, CBRN Operations; DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.

3. **Effective Date.** [DD MONTH YYYY].

4. **Period.** Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.

5. **Duties and Responsibilities.** The CBRN Primary will:
   - Serve as the commander's principal enlisted advisor on CBRN matters
   - Manage the unit CBRN program and training
   - Maintain accountability of CBRN equipment
   - Conduct CBRN equipment maintenance and serviceability inspections
   - Ensure CBRN equipment is properly stored and maintained
   - Coordinate CBRN training requirements and scheduling
   - Maintain CBRN training records and documentation
   - Ensure personnel maintain current CBRN certifications
   - Coordinate with installation CBRN office
   - Brief commander on CBRN readiness status

6. **Qualifications.** Must hold MOS 74D or have completed equivalent CBRN training certification.

7. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.

8. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].
