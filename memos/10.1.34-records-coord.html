MEMORANDUM FOR SEE DISTRIBUTION

SUBJECT: Unit Records Coordinator Appointment

  1. In accordance with AR 25-400-2 and DA Pam 750-1, the following individuals are appointed as the Unit Records Coordinator for [UNIT NAME].

a. Primary: [RANK, NAME]

b. Alternate: [RANK, NAME]

  1. Authority. AR 25-400-2, The Army Records Information Management System (ARIMS); DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.
  1. Effective Date. [DD MONTH YYYY].
  1. Period. Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.
  1. Duties and Responsibilities. The Unit Records Coordinator will:
  • Manage unit records in accordance with AR 25-400-2
  • Ensure proper maintenance of equipment records
  • Coordinate with GCSS-Army for electronic records management
  • Maintain DA Form 2408 series and other maintenance documentation
  • Ensure records are properly filed and retrievable
  • Conduct periodic reviews of unit records
  • Coordinate records disposition and destruction
  • Train personnel on records management procedures
  1. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.
  1. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].

Respectfully,

[LAST NAME, FIRST NAME MI.]

[RANK, BRANCH, COMPONENT]

Commanding
