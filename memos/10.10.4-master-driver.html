MEMORANDUM FOR SEE DISTRIBUTION

SUBJECT: Master Driver Appointment

  1. In accordance with AR 600-55 and DA Pam 750-1, the following individuals are appointed as the Master Driver for [UNIT NAME].

a. Primary: [RANK, NAME]

b. Alternate: [RANK, NAME]

  1. Authority. AR 600-55, The Army Driver and Operator Standardization Program (Selection, Training, Testing, and Licensing); DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.
  1. Effective Date. [DD MONTH YYYY].
  1. Period. Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.
  1. Duties and Responsibilities. The Master Driver will:
  • Manage the unit driver training and licensing program
  • Supervise License Instructors and License Examiners
  • Develop and maintain driver training schedules and plans
  • Ensure operator training meets AR 600-55 requirements
  • Review and validate operator licensing packets
  • Conduct quarterly reviews of unit licensing program
  • Maintain driver training records and documentation
  • Coordinate sustainment training for licensed operators
  • Brief commander on driver training program status
  1. Qualifications. Must be a licensed operator on all equipment types assigned to the unit and have completed Master Driver training.
  1. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.
  1. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].

Respectfully,

[LAST NAME, FIRST NAME MI.]

[RANK, BRANCH, COMPONENT]

Commanding
