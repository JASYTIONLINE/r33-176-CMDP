SUBJECT: License Instructor Appointment

1. In accordance with AR 600-55 and DA Pam 750-1, the following individuals are appointed as License Instructors for [UNIT NAME].

   a. [RANK, NAME] — Licensed on: [EQUIPMENT TYPES]  
   b. [RANK, NAME] — Licensed on: [EQUIPMENT TYPES]  
   c. [RANK, NAME] — Licensed on: [EQUIPMENT TYPES]

2. **Authority.** AR 600-55, The Army Driver and Operator Standardization Program (Selection, Training, Testing, and Licensing); DA Pam 750-1, Chapter 10.

3. **Effective Date.** [DD MONTH YYYY].

4. **Period.** Indefinite or until formally relieved in writing.

5. **Duties and Responsibilities.** License Instructors will:
   - Conduct hands-on operator training IAW AR 600-55
   - Train operators on proper PMCS procedures
   - Instruct operators on vehicle/equipment operation and safety
   - Document training in operator training records
   - Prepare operators for licensing examinations
   - Ensure training meets all regulatory requirements
   - Coordinate training schedules with Master Driver
   - Maintain proficiency on assigned equipment

6. **Qualifications.** Must be a licensed operator on all equipment types they instruct and have completed License Instructor training.

7. This appointment memorandum will be validated annually.

8. The point of contact for this memorandum is [RANK NAME], [DUTY POSITION], at [PHONE] or [EMAIL].
